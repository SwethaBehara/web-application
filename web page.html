Stay Safe in the Digital World

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These attacks often aim to access, change, or destroy sensitive information, extort money, or interrupt normal processes.

Best Practices

  • Use strong and unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Be cautious with suspicious emails & links
  • Keep your software up to date
  • Use antivirus and firewall protection

Common Threats

  • Phishing Attacks
  • Ransomware
  • Malware
  • Data Breaches
